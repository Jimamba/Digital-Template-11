Blue Square vs. Red Circle

  • High Concept: It's a boss battle between the player and a red circle.
  • Player Experience Goals: I want my players to enjoy the fight as if it were a boss battle in a much bigger game.
  • Genre: Boss Battle Plat-former.
  • Game play: The player can move up, left, and right using the arrow keys. They can fire using the F key, with their shots coming out a bit delayed. The Red Circle can fire shots back, however it's shots are not limited to just its body. The shots fire from multiple heights depending on the player.
  • Prototype goal: Boss Battles. I wanted to put in more attacks, but ran into trouble with killing them after a certain time has passed.
  • Features: The bullets the boss fires are created using the player's height rather than the boss' height.
  • Setting/Premise: The player is a blue square fighting a red circle.
  • Story: How will win and how will lose in this battle between geometric shapes? Only you can decide.
  • Target Audience: People who like plat-formers.
  • Play time: Approximately a minute and a half.
  • Strategies: Don't stay on one floor, swap between both.
